Privacy, plainly

Plain-language summary. A full attorney-reviewed policy will replace this page before public beta.

What CourtBeat stores

Your first name, last initial, self-rated skill level, and court-level presence events — "checked in at Surprise Community Park at 9:02" — plus rallies you join.

What we never store

No GPS traces. Location is checked at the moment you tap Check in (and, if you allow Always access, when you leave the park). Coordinates are discarded — only the at-the-park event is kept.

Who sees you

Other signed-in players see your first name, last initial, skill level, reliability tier, and founder number. Anonymous visitors to this site see player counts only — never names.

No selling, no ads

Your data runs the app. It is not sold or shared with advertisers.

Deleting your account

In the app: Profile → Delete account. Everything is erased server-side immediately — profile, presence history, rallies, founder number, all of it.

Age

CourtBeat is for players 13 and up.
